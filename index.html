What is Rummy? A Quick Refresher

Rummy is a group of matching-card games that involve forming valid sets and sequences. The goal is to arrange the cards in your hand into combinations such as:

  • A sequence (3 or more cards in a row of the same suit)
  • A set (3 or 4 cards of the same rank but different suits)
